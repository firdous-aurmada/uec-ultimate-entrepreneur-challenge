HOW TO PLAY

🎯 THE GOAL

Win 2 of 3 rounds. Empty your rival's health bar or have more health when the 60-second bell rings. Landing and taking hits charges your energy meter — spend it on your Special (50) or go full UNICORN MODE (100).

⌨️ CONTROLS — SOLO
On phones & tablets, on-screen pads appear automatically.
👥 CONTROLS — LOCAL 2-PLAYER
🛡 DEFENSE & METER

Block (hold) cuts damage to 15% chip. You can't block a Hostile Takeover — jump away instead. Energy: +12 when you land a hit, +8 when you take one. Specials cost 50. Unicorn Mode costs a full 100 bar and lasts 6 seconds: +35% damage, +25% speed and no chip damage taken.

⚡ SPECIAL ABILITIES
🏆 RANKED POINTS

Solo AI fights and friend challenges are ranked: win +20 × difficulty (Intern ×1 · Founder ×1.5 · Mogul ×2.5 · Challenges ×2) plus +5 per KO round and a streak bonus. A loss still earns +3 for showing up — very lean-startup of you.
