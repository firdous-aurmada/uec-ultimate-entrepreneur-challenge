HOW TO PLAY

🎯 THE GOAL

Win 2 of 3 rounds. Empty your rival's health bar or have more health when the 60-second bell rings. Landing and taking hits charges your energy meter — spend it on a ⚖️ Cease & Desist (25), your ⚡ Special (50) or go full 🦄 UNICORN MODE (100) — or skip the meter entirely and 💸 Acqui-Hire their energy for yourself. And race your rival to the 💼 mystery briefcases that fall mid-round — every one hides a secret power.

⌨️ CONTROLS — SOLO
On phones & tablets, on-screen pads appear automatically.
👥 CONTROLS — LOCAL 2-PLAYER
🔗 COMBOS

Attacks cancel on hit: chain Punch ×3 → Kick → Special / ⚖️ / 🦄 — or sneak a 💸 Acqui-Hire into the string. Whiffed swings still recover in full — no mashing your way to safety. Chained hits deal scaled-down damage the deeper you go, so save the expensive finishers for the end of a string and watch the HITS counter climb.

🛡 DEFENSE & METER

Block (hold) cuts damage to 15% chip — and chip can never finish you. You can't block a Hostile Takeover — jump away instead. 💨 Dash (free, short cooldown) closes gaps or escapes corners and cancels straight into attacks. PARRY: tap block at the very last instant to turn an attack away — the attacker staggers and you pocket energy. Grabs beat parries. Energy: +12 when you land a hit, +8 when you take one — spend it 💣 25 · ⚡ 50 · 🦄 100. Unicorn Mode lasts 6 seconds: +35% damage, +25% speed, no chip taken. A 🛡 Legal Shield briefcase absorbs one full hit — even a Takeover.

⚡ SPECIAL ABILITIES
🔴 PLAY WITH FRIENDS

Live match: Challenges → Create Live Room → send the link. You'll be pinged the second your rival joins, you each pick a fighter (host picks the arena), and you fight in real time — rematches fire when both players accept. Async call-out: share your challenge link any time and friends battle your fighter's AI ghost whenever they open it. Every share button on the results screen doubles as a call-out — brag responsibly.

🏆 RANKED POINTS

Solo AI fights, call-outs and live matches are ranked: win +20 × difficulty (Intern ×1 · Founder ×1.5 · Mogul ×2.5 · Call-outs & Live ×2) plus +5 per KO round and a streak bonus. A loss still earns +3 for showing up — very lean-startup of you.
